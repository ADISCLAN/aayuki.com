Our Foundation

Engineered for Global Markets

Based in Rajkot's Vavdi Industrial Area — India's foremost precision manufacturing hub — Aayuki Export has built a reputation on three pillars: material quality, dimensional accuracy, and reliable supply chains.

Every product in our range is manufactured to the exact specifications required by international markets. Our facility handles everything from raw material selection through to final QC inspection, carton packaging and export documentation.

Our Journey

How We Grew

Every milestone is a testament to the trust our partners have placed in us — and our commitment to never stop improving.

2000
Founded in Rajkot
Established as a precision PVC fittings manufacturer in Vavdi Industrial Area with a focus on SWR drainage products.
2005
First Export Contract
Secured first international export contract supplying BS4514-compliant SWR fittings to the UK market.
2010
PP Fittings Range Launch
Expanded product range to include full PPCP PP fittings in 32 MM and 40 MM, tripling our catalogue.
2015
Floor Trap & Shower Products
Launched premium floor trap range with PVD Gold, Rose and Black finishing technology — entering the luxury bathroom accessories market.
2020
30+ Markets Milestone
Reached the landmark of supplying to over 30 countries across Europe, Africa, Middle East and Asia.
2024
AI-Powered Customer Experience
Launched our Claude-powered AI product assistant, delivering instant, accurate answers to global customers 24/7.
